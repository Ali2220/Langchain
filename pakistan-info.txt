Pakistan ki economy agriculture par based hai. Cotton, wheat aur rice important crops hain.
Industry sector mein textiles, cement aur sugar mills hain. IT sector rapidly grow kar raha hai.
Freelancing mein Pakistan 4th number par hai. Karachi, Lahore aur Islamabad IT hubs hain.
Government ne Digital Pakistan initiative start ki hai. 2025 tak IT exports $15 billion ka target hai.
Pakistan ki population 24 crore se zyada hai. Youth population 60% hai.
Cricket sabse popular sport hai. Hockey national sport hai.
